Rupee Collector

Instructions


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game called "Number To Get"
  2. There are four rupees below.
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow

  3. By clicking on a rupee, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the Number to Get.
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. The value of each rupee will be hidden until it is clicked on.
  7. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each rupee.
